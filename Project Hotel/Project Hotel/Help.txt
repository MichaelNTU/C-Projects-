Functional testing:
This system is design to output list of Menus, Rooms, 
make a reservation for room, 
make an order for meal, and then display staff file. 
And calculate wages and tax, 
and then deduct the taxes from the wages. 
And again display it on the screen both 
monthly and annually net-wages and gross-wages. 
This is for workers who are paid hourly. 
The system   also can connect to the internet by pressing 4,
for price comparison if costumer is not satisfied with 
the available price, again, if the costumer wants to view 
their booking on the screen press 5.
